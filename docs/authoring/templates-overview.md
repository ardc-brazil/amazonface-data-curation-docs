# Documentation Templates

Use these templates when creating new operational chapters and SOP documents.

## Available Templates

- `chapter-template.md`: Full chapter structure for standards and governance sections.
- `sop-template.md`: Procedure-oriented template for runbooks and repeatable operations.

## Authoring Rules

- Keep scope explicit (`In Scope` and `Out of Scope`).
- Always include roles, controls, and required evidence.
- Add measurable quality controls and failure actions.
- Keep change history updated for auditability.
